My Story

My life and experiences are as unique as my name. Born and raised in Pakistan moved to the US to go to college and learn and build a life that i could be extremely proud of. One of my biggest strengths that also makes me a good UX designer is that I like to solve problems. My problems, your problems and any UX problems, throw them at me and by the end of the week they’ll be solved. (I’m serious).The reason all problems are solved is because I enjoy digging deep and getting into the nitti gritty details. With 3 years of Package Design experience I developed a loving relationship with the design world and have acquired skills that will help me in all design aspects for example team work time sensative projects, client designer interaction/relationship, problem solving (oh yeah), packaging files to hand off to other designers/developers, user research, competitive analysis etc. When Im not designing you can find me organizing/color coding anything in my house or listening to true crime podcasts or maybe doing both at the same time. I enjoy hanging out with friends and family. As a New Yorker i enjoy finding delicious desserts in town and little hidden gems like bars, bookstores restaurants etc .
